Joanna’s Shower in 5 Steps

Introduce Yourself

“Hi, I am…” Be welcoming, engage in small talk ask her how her day was. You want to make her comfortable around you. When she is comfortable tell her your going to help her take a shower.

Setting up the bathroom

Before you take Joanna to the bathroom make sure the mirror is covered, Stayin’ Alive by the Bee Gees is playing and the shower temperature is set to warm. Her reflection scares her and the Bee Gees will calm her down as she showers. Expect some singing!

Approach Joanna

Approach Joanna slowly and engage in some small talk. If she is watching TV ask her what she’s watching. Once you have a conversation tell her your hands are dirty and if she wants to walk with you to the bathroom so you can wash them. Once at the bathroom as her if she wants to shower.

Shower time

Help Joanna into the shower by holding her hand as she climbs in. It helps to talk to her as you do this. Once she is in the shower assist her in washing up she loves using lavendar scented shampoo.

Getting Dressed

Joanna enjoys choosing her outfit. Pick out three outfits for her and let het choose one from the three. Help her put on her choice.
