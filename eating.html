Breakfast, Lunch or Dinner in 5 Steps

Introduce Yourself

“Hey, my name is…” Be welcoming, engage in small talk ask her how her day was. You want to make her comfortable around you. When she is comfortable tell her your going to have a meal with her.

Set up the table

Before you take Joanna to the table make sure the table is set, make sure there are no reflective objects and the meal is nice and warm. Also, place all utencils to the right it makes it easier for her to use due to her eye condition

Approach Joanna

Approach Joanna slowly and engage in some small talk. If she is watching TV ask her what she’s watching. Once you get a conversation started ask her if she would like to continue the conversation over a meal. She loves to have a conversation and eat her meal.

Meal time

Help Joanna by placing the food in her plate, place close attention to her hand gestures that's how she signals how much is enough. If she doesn't feel like eating talk to her, it gives her an apetite!

Cleaning Up

Once the meal is over ask her what she thought of it. She loves talking about food especially if it's Italian. Once you have this conversation ask if she would like to wash her hands.
