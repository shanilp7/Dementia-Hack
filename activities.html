Make Joanna's Day in 5 Steps

Introduce Yourself

“Hi, Joanna my name's...” Be welcoming, engage in small talk ask her what she thinks of the weather. You want to make her comfortable around you. When she is comfortable ask her if she wants to go to the park. She loves walks in the park!

Planning the route

Before you take Joanna to the park make sure you know the route you will take. She easily scares at the sight of her reflection. Also pick the route that's less hectic. She enjoys peace and quite.

Take her to the park

Hold Joanna's hand at all times, she is scared to fall and sometimes misjudges her steps. While you hold her hand make small talk to keep her occupied. She also tends to wonder so keep your eye one her and stay close!

Walk in the Park

Joanna likes walking past the pond and looking at the geese. Be careful, don't take her too close to the water the reflection will agitate her. She also likes to sit on the park bench near the large oak tree.

Walking Back

Joanna may be exhausted so be extra aware, she might complain about feeling dizzy so take the time to listen to her. She may also feel excited, yet another reason to be aware and keep her calm by engaging in small talk.
